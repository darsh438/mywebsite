Kerala Local Body Election Results: Vote counting is underway for the Kerala local body elections. Early trends indicate the Congress-led UDF (United Democratic Front) is surging towards victory across the state. However, in a "historic" win, the BJP-led NDA (National Democratic Alliance) secured the Thiruvananthapuram Municipal Corporation, a long-time Left bastion.

2001 Parliament Attack Anniversary: Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Vice President C.P. Radhakrishnan, and other Parliamentarians paid floral tributes to the security personnel who lost their lives in the 2001 Parliament attack.

Delhi Air Quality 'Severe': Anti-pollution curbs under Stage III of the Graded Response Action Plan (GRAP-3) have been imposed in Delhi as the air quality index (AQI) worsened to the 'severe' category due to dense fog and smog.
