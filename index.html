National & Political Updates

Census 2027 Phase I: The government has officially notified the first phase of Census 2027, specifically houselisting operations, to take place across all states and UTs between April 1 and September 30, 2026.

Voter Roll Purge (SIR 2.0): Nearly 6.5 crore electors have been removed from draft rolls in nine states and three UTs as part of the "Summary Intelligence Revision" to ensure electoral purity.

ED Raids on TMC: West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee has condemned Enforcement Directorate (ED) raids at the residence of the Trinamool Congress (TMC) IT head, labeling them "political vendetta".

Indore Water Crisis: A major political row continues in Madhya Pradesh following a water contamination tragedy in Indore. While officials initially reported 4-8 deaths, administration records now show compensation has been distributed to 18 families.

Vaishno Devi Medical College Row: The J&K administration has sharply criticized the National Medical Commission (NMC) for withdrawing recognition of Mata Vaishno Devi Medical College, calling the process "flawed and predetermined".

Foreign Affairs & Global Impact

US Sanctions Threat: US President Donald Trump has reportedly "greenlit" a Russia Sanctions Bill that could impose tariffs of up to 500% on India and China for continuing to purchase Russian oil.

India-Israel Ties: Prime Minister Modi recently spoke with Israeli PM Benjamin Netanyahu, who provided an update on a potential Gaza Peace Plan and discussed strengthening bilateral security.

Border Security: A large-scale combing operation is ongoing in the Kathua forests of Jammu and Kashmir following a recent encounter between security forces and terrorists.

Economy & Environment

GDP Projections: India’s real GDP growth for FY 2025–26 is estimated at 7.4% by the National Statistical Office, a rise from 6.5% the previous year.

Winter Weather: North India is reeling under a severe cold wave. Delhi recorded its third-lowest temperature of the season at 5.8°C today, with dense fog leading to an orange alert for Rajasthan, Haryana, and Punjab.

Ecologist Madhav Gadgil Passes Away: Renowned ecologist and champion of Western Ghats conservation, Madhav Gadgil, passed away in Pune at the age of 83.

Sports & Entertainment

T20 World Cup 2026: The ICC has rejected the Bangladesh Cricket Board's (BCB) request to move its matches out of India, issuing an ultimatum that the team must play in India or forfeit points.

Cricket Achievements: 14-year-old prodigy Vaibhav Suryavanshi hit a 63-ball century for India U19 against South Africa.

Cinema Delay: The release of the film Jana Nayagan, starring Vijay, has been postponed, leading to a massive social media rally by fans.
